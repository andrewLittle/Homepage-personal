  • Abercrombie & Fitch

    User Interface Technical Lead

    At Abercrombie and Fitch I am a Technical Lead for the Front End Engineering team. I have responsibilities across all areas of the website for all four of Abercrombie's brands, Abercrombie and Fitch, Abercrombie Kids, Hollister, and Gilly Hicks. We use JSPs to create our markup, have started the process of transferring our styling from vanilla CSS to Sass, and use JQuery as well as other plugins for our interaction functionality. I provide input during the requirements gathering process to the design and business teams, develop to the specifications of the requirements, support Quality Assurance and Customer Acceptance Testing, and through code reviews provide guidence to other team members on our coding standards.

  • IBM
